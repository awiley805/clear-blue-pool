Pool Renovations

Clear Blue Pools offers complete resurfacing solutions for both residential and commercial pools and spas. We have several options when it comes to resurfacing including plaster, quartz, and pebble. Aries Pools also offers a wide range of tile options designed to best fit your requirements.

If your pool and spa is in need of resurfacing call Clear Blue Pools today. We will schedule an appointment for you to meet with one of our resurfacing team members who will work with you one on one to find an optimum solution for pool and spa resurfacing needs. Aries Pools prides itself by working individually with each customer so that we can best understand your requirements and preferences so that we can find and present a design plan tailored specifically to your needs. We work with our customers to ensure we stay within your budget.

Clear Blue Pools offers free estimates with no obligation so call us today and schedule an appointment!

    Outdoor Living

    Clear Blue Pools has been building outdoor living spaces for over 15 years! We truly love to create unique outdoor spaces that will show off the true beauty of our property and will letting home owners make the most of their yards all year round. We work with new construction and modern homes, as well as historic homes.

    Our outdoor living services allow you to customize your yard to create the types of spaces you truly love, need, and want. When thinking of ways to get more use from your yard talk to those who not only understand how to create functional outdoor spaces but also how to customize these spaces to reflect your needs, budget, and style.

    Pit Pits and Fire Features

    Water and fire are two of the most calming and mesmerizing elements on the planet. Let Clear Blue Pools integrate one or both of these features into a new or existing backyard design.

    From small fountains to multi-tiered, stacked stone waterfalls, the soothing sound of water adds dimension to any projects. Integrated with lighting, water features can provide stunning visual effects. Fire is another popular feature, adding year-round warmth, comfort and beauty. These beautiful eatures will instantly transform any outdoor space.

    Build a New Custom Pool or Spa

    Let us design and build your dream pool!

    Building a new pool should be fun, not stressful. At Clear Blue Pools, we’ve built hundreds of pools over the last 15 years and have some of the highest industry and customer ratings in Texas.

    Our pools are professionally designed, starting with a thorough assessment of your yard space, surroundings and vision. We always consider the style of your home and account for changes in elevations and landscapes. We’ll work with you to choose the right design, colors and materials for your space, resulting in a pool as unique as you are.

  • Fully-automated pool and accessory control systems
