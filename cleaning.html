Pool Cleaning Service

Clear Blue Pools offers complete cleaning service solutions for your pool and spa. For your convenience we offer a flat monthly rate with chemicals included in the total cost.

When you sign up for weekly service a dedicated uniformed Clear Blue Pools Technician Team Member will come to your residence once a week on the day of your convenience and perform the cleaning service of your preference.

Clear Blue Pools currently offers full weekly pool cleaning service, chemical only service, and one time cleaning only service.

With our Weekly Pool and One Time Only Cleaning Service a dedicated Uniformed Technician's will service your pool once a week performing the following services:

  • Skim pool surface of all leaves and debris.
  • Brushing all stairs, handrails, and sides of the pool and spa.
  • Vacuum pool and spa floor.
  • Empty all skimmer and pump baskets.
  • Empty Automatic Cleaner Net.
  • Conduct water chemistry analysis.
  • add appropriate chemicals as required.
  • Check salt levels and add salt as appropriate.
  • Backwash Filter when necessary.
  • Conduct visual inspection of equipment to ensure working order.
